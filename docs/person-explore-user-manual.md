# Person Explore User Manual

## Overview

The person explore workspace gives contributors a focused place to review and maintain a person's:

- educational history
- professional history
- living history
- life history album

Open it from the `Explore` link on a person card.

## Overview Tab

The `Overview` tab summarizes:

- education entries
- professional roles
- latest professional position
- counts for residences and life-history photos

## Living History Tab

Use the `Living History` tab to maintain residential records.

Each residence can include:

- place name
- address
- start date
- end date
- notes
- latitude
- longitude

Use `Open map` to view the residence location in an external map search.

## Life History Tab

Use the `Life History` tab to upload and organize photos.

Each album item supports:

- portrait or group category
- photo date
- age period label
- location
- description
- people tags

## Permissions

- viewers can explore and read information
- editors can update living history and upload or remove album photos
- photo access is protected by graph membership permissions
